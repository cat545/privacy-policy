Privacy Policy for EXDV.DEV applications and Website

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email exdv.dev (at) gmail.com.

At our website pozdrav.su and in our mobile applications we consider the privacy of our visitors to be extremely important. This privacy policy document describes in detail the types of personal information collected and recorded by pozdrav.su and our mobile applications and how we use it.

Cookies and Web Beacons

pozdrav.su and our mobile application use cookies to store information about visitors’ preferences, record user-specific information on accessed pages, and personalize web page content based on visitors’ browser type or other information.

Applications permissions and how we use them

  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE – to save our database to your device’s external memory
  • CHECK_LICENSE – to verify app license
  • INTERNET – to download database, display ads
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – to check internet connection
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE – to detect Wi-Fi or cellular connection
  • ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION – for location-based ads
  • READ_PHONE_STATE – required by some Ad SDKs
  • WAKE_LOCK – to prevent sleep mode while downloading
  • BILLING – for in-app purchases
